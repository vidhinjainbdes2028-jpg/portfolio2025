STUDIO GHIBLI RECIPE BOOK

Tales from the Ghibli Table is a handcrafted recipe book reimagining iconic Studio Ghibli meals. Blending nostalgia, watercolor illustrations, and comforting recipes, it captures the warmth of a grandmother’s journal while offering a magical, family-friendly cooking experience.

First Prototype

The first prototype was handmade with rough paper and uncoloured sketches for a raw, sketchbook feel. Based on feedback, the final version used better paper, coloured illustrations, and digitally printed text for improved readability and a refined finish.
